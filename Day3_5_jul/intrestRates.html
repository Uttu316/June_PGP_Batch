FD Interest Rates

One of the best ways to secure your money is by investing in fixed deposits which not only helps you to save money but also helps you to earn a substantial interest in it. Competitive Bank FD interest rates help the depositor to get a good Return on Investment over a fixed tenure. Under the fixed deposit scheme, the depositor deposits the money only once at the time of opening the account. The interest rates offereddepends on the bank , deposit amount and the tenure you choose.
